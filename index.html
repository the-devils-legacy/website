WELCOME

We are an all inclusive gaming community with the desire to find mature, like minded gamers. Our primary focus is Destiny 2 with plans to expand into other games.

RULES

All members are expected to read and understand our rules.

  • No Nudity, Gore, Racist, Sexist, Violent, Disturbing, Inappropriate, or Offensive text, voice, images, or videos anywhere in our Community. (Attempting to circumvent these rules through direct message, custom status, profile picture, and/or nickname is not allowed.)
  • Be respectful to all members of the community.
  • Please keep conflict out of the community, this extends to DMs.
  • Follow instructions from all our staff. Any issues with a Officer or Moderator, can be directed to an Admin.
  • Advertisement of another Discord, Social Media, or Community Websites are not allowed and will result in a ban.
  • Never impersonate staff or another member of the Community.
  • Keep all political discussion out of the community.
